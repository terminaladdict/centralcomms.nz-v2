10 Gigabit Ethernet (10GE, 10GbE, or 10 GigE) is here — and it makes your network rocket! 🚀

We're experts in fibre splicing and copper structured data cabling. Be it cabling for your CCTV system, your phone system, or your general network infrastructure — we've got the cabling solution for you.

![Cabinet installation](/assets/images/posts/thumbnails/20220604_090014.jpg)

For this installation, we set up the primary cabinet with SFP+ ports for 10Gb fibre uplinks and 10GBase-T for cabled Cat6 10Gb devices.

![Secondary cabinet with DAC cables](/assets/images/posts/thumbnails/20220606_160901.jpg)

The secondary cabinet runs DAC cable from the primary cabinet using the SFP+ ports, then distributes via Gigabit PoE and Gigabit non-PoE switches throughout the network.
