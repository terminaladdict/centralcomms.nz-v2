All the data that customers generate and entrust to us is backed up in Hamilton, our main location. In August 2020 we set up an offsite backup in Mount Maunganui. There are worse places in the world to visit your backup service! We think New Zealand is pretty lucky having beaches like Mount Maunganui. We now have a complete snapshot of all our data stored on servers in Mount Maunganui as well, giving our customers true geographic redundancy.
