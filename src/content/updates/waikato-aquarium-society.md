Established over 50 years ago, the Waikato Aquarium Society (WAS) is a club for people in the Waikato region who keep aquariums and enjoy the hobby of fishkeeping. They wanted a site where people could register to sell their fish. We designed and developed a system for their members to list and sell fish (and anything else!) online.
