In 2016 Dance Connection started providing modern jive dance classes, and wanted to share their classes with the world. They wanted to host a video-based site where people could watch videos of the dance classes. Together, we built a great website integrated tightly with YouTube, allowing Dance Connection to upload their videos and have them appear automatically on their website.
