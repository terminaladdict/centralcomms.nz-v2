We've been back in the local freezing works. This time we've been running cables for additional workstations. It's a tough job getting cables through tight spots in some roofs and under some floors. But we are experienced experts at structured cabling. There are not many places we can't get a cable (copper or fibre). And if we can't get a cable run, we've got expert wireless radio engineers who can get data just about anywhere.
