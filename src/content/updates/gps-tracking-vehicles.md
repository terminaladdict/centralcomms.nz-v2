Once upon a time Paul owned an expensive Harley Davidson Fatbob. One night while thinking about security he started thinking about GPS trackers. One thing led to another and Paul worked out a system to track vehicles.

Open source to the rescue! Traccar is an open source GPS tracking software. You need to configure an Android phone to get it going, but it's well worth it when you want to look after your ride.

![Harley Davidson Fatbob](/assets/images/posts/thumbnails/Fatbob_right_side.jpg)

Traccar gives you real-time vehicle location, history, and alerts — all self-hosted and under your control.
